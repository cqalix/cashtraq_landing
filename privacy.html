Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal data when you use Cashtraq.

What Data We Collect

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, and login credentials when you register.
  • Usage Data: Information on how you use the app, including clicks and preferences.
  • Cookies: Small data files stored on your device to improve your experience.

How We Use Your Data

We use your data to:

  • Provide and personalise your experience
  • Manage your account and authentication
  • Improve our services based on usage
  • Communicate important updates and offers

Legal Basis for Processing

We process your data based on your consent and our legitimate interests in providing and improving the service.

Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, loss, or misuse.

Sharing Your Data

We do not sell your data. We may share data with trusted service providers (e.g., analytics, hosting) who help us run Cashtraq securely.

Your Rights

You have rights under UK GDPR, including to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request correction or deletion
  • Withdraw consent at any time
  • Object to data processing
  • Receive a copy of your data in portable format

To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@cashtraq.com.

Cookies

We use cookies to enhance your experience. You can manage or disable cookies via your browser settings. We also use a cookie consent banner on our website.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this policy or your data, please contact us at privacy@cashtraq.com.
