Cashtraq Help Assistant

Where is my cashback?
Cashback can take time to appear depending on the retailer. Most track within 72 hours, but some retailers take several weeks — even up to 90 days — to confirm transactions.
Why hasn’t my cashback tracked?
Tracking may fail due to ad blockers, cookies being disabled, or if you didn’t click directly through Cashtraq to the retailer. This is normal for some merchants.
How long does cashback take to confirm?
Retailers confirm cashback within a few days to 3 months, depending on return policies and transaction types.
How do loyalty tiers work?
We offer Bronze, Silver, and Gold tiers based on total cashback earned. More cashback = higher tier and better perks in the future.
Is my data secure?
Yes. We use secure storage, encryption, and never sell your data to third parties.
